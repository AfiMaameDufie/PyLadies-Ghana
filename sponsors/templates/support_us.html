Why we need sponsors

Python Ghana Ladies is wholly organized by volunteers. We therefore need sponsors to help us cover the costs of:

    -
  • basic running expenses;
    • -
  • provide a decent location to host Meetups;
    • -
  • reimburse the travel and accommodation costs of our speakers;
    • -
  • provide financial assistance to participant who cannot otherwise afford to attend the Meetups;
    • -
  • catering and refreshments;
    • -
  • educational materials for the tutorials;
    • -
  • video recording of talks!
